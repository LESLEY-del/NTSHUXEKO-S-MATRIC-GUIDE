MATHS

Since you just arrived in Matric there are a few things you must know.

  1. In Paper 1 and Paper 2 there are easy and difficult questions.
  2. Some work continues from Grade 11.
  3. With what you know from last year you already have a strong base.

Important topics:

  • Algebra (20-30 marks)
  • Number Patterns (20-25 marks)
  • Functions (20-33 marks)
  • Probability (8-12 marks)
  • Financial Maths (8-12 marks)

Your pass mark is already close with these topics.

PHYSICAL SCIENCE

Physical Science is closely related to Mathematics because both involve calculations. Practise them together to improve understanding.

Paper 1 Topics:

  • Multiple Choice (20 marks)
  • Definitions
  • Mechanics (40-50 marks)
  • Waves, Sound & Light (20-30 marks)
  • Electric Circuits (30-35 marks)

Paper 2 Topics:

  • Multiple Choice
  • Definitions
  • Organic Chemistry (40-50 marks)
  • Electrochemistry (18-26 marks)
  • Acids and Bases (20-30 marks)

GEOGRAPHY / LIFE SCIENCES

These subjects require understanding and memorising processes. Study the concepts first before attempting question papers.

After studying, practise using past papers and check your answers using the memo.

HOME LANGUAGES

Home languages are usually easier to pass if you read regularly and practise comprehension and writing.

LIFE ORIENTATION

Life Orientation is important. It can help you pass or fail. Study it properly and take it seriously.
